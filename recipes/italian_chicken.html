Crockpot Italian Chicken

Description

A "throw it in and forget about it" crockpot italian chicken recipe as detailed by LeAnn Haas Gabriel on Allrecipes! With only THREE ingredients, this recipe will be perfect for those lazy Sundays and will still be sure to impress. This recipe yields 4 servings and will take 8 hours of unattended cooking time. Just set the crockpot and forget about it!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions

  1. Stir Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on Low until chicken is tender, no longer pink in the center, and the juices run clear, about 8 hours.
